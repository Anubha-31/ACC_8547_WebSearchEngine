Master of Business Administration or MBA is one of the most popular post-graduate programmes in Mumbai. The two-year programme is a gateway to a plethora of job opportunities in the corporate world. th More than 50 MBA colleges in Hyderabad offering quality education, make the city a cherished dream of pursuing management education from here. First it was ICFAI Business School that was set up in 1990s, and then it was Indian School of Business (ISB) in 2001 that put Hyderabad on the map of Indian management education. Today, Hyderabad has many good MBA colleges like IMT Hyderabad, VJIM, IPE, Vishwa Vishwani among others. MBA in Hyderabad is broadly offered by three types of MBA colleges, namely IIMs – IIM Visakhapatnam; AICTE-Approved PGDM Colleges in Hyderabad like IPE, VJIM, MANAGE, IMT, GITAM and university MBA colleges like ICFAI Business School (IBS), SIBM, GITAM-HBS. All these management institutes make Hyderabad a preferred MBA destination. An MBA professional must possess qualities like excellent communication, leadership, problem-solving, management, research-oriented, analytical thinking, eye for detail, goal-oriented & ability to work under pressure, strong mathematical skills, etc. In Hyderabad, is offered across various specialisations. Some of the most prominent ones are as follows: MBA degree Finance Sales and Marketing Human Resources Operations IT and Systems Healthcare and Hospital Business Analytics International Business Agriculture and Food Business Retail The eligibility criteria for MBA course may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for MBA is as follows: Candidates must have passed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university as a basic eligibility criteria for MBA (full-time). Most of the institutes follow the minimum score criteria in graduation which is 50% in average or equivalent. For reserved category students, minimum score is 45% in aggregate. Final-year graduation candidates are also eligible to apply for MBA, provided they present the proof of completion of graduation degree within the duration specified by the institute. There are approximately 224 MBA colleges in Hyderabad which offer MBA courses, out of these 104 are private, 21 are public and rest are public-private.
