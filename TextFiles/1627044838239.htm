India 2021 Private Master of Business Administration (MBA) is one of the most popular post-graduate programmes in India and abroad. The two-year MBA programme acts as a gateway to numerous job and entrepreneurial opportunities. The course provides an understanding of the administration of the business and managing business operations. Students from any background, such as Science, Commerce and Humanities can pursue Private MBA. Such students should also possess skills such as excellent communication, leadership, problem-solving, management, research-oriented, analytical thinking, eye for detail, goal-oriented & ability to work under pressure, strong mathematical skills, etc. The full-time Private MBA is the most popular among all and includes theory classes, practical projects, and internship. Fresh graduates as well as candidates with a few years of work experience opt for a full-time Private MBA. India is home to some of the top-rated Private MBA colleges to pursue a . These include XLRI Xavier School of Management (Jamshedpur), Management Development Institute (MDI Gurgaon), National Institute of Industrial Engineering (NITIE Mumbai), S.P. These colleges provide degree, diploma and certificate courses. Out of these the top 20 Private MBA colleges in India ranked by NIRF, Outlook and Times offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Private MBA Admission Eligibility Criteria The eligibility criteria for a Private MBA course may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria to pursue a Private MBA is as follows: Candidates must have passed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university as a basic eligibility criteria for Private MBA (full-time) Most of the institutes follow the minimum score criteria in graduation which is 50 per cent on average or equivalent. Some of the colleges also offer a five per cent relaxation to students from the reserved categories.
