Fashion Design Colleges in India 2021 Fashion Designing is considered as one of the most exciting and demanded career options in India. A thorough career in Fashion Designing cannot be achieved only with academics. It requires a deep passion for fabric, inner talents, styling and designing, and industrial exposure, etc. Fashion Design colleges in India render the dual advantage of rich industrial exposure-cum-creative designing lessons which are of world-class standards. Aspirants can pursue Fashion Design as a course at the undergraduate or even at the postgraduate level. Apart from this, aspirants can even pursue short-term Diploma or Certificate programmes in the field of Fashion Design. In order to become a Fashion Designer one should consider pursuing a bachelor’s degree in design. Some popular courses that candidates can pursue in design to become a Fashion Designer include BDes in Fashion Design, Bachelor of Fashion Technology (BFTech), BSc in Fashion Design, Fashion Merchandising, etc. Apart from these courses candidates can also pursue Fashion Design courses at the PG level like Master of Design (MDes), Masters of Fashion Technology (MFTech), etc. India is home to some of the top-rated Fashion Design institutes such as National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Delhi; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Bangalore; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Chennai; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Patna; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Gandhinagar; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Hyderabad; Pearl Academy, Delhi; National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Kolkata; Symbiosis Institute of Design, Pune; Pearl Academy, Jaipur, etc. Some of the popular specialisations offered by Fashion Design colleges in India are: Fashion Design Jewellery Design Lifestyle Accessory Design Apparel Design Textile Design Visual Merchandising Knitwear Design There are a total of over 1,100 Fashion Design colleges in India that provide a full-time, part-time, distance-learning and online mode of study. These colleges provide degree courses. Out of these, the top 20 Fashion Design colleges in India ranked by India Today offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Some of the colleges offer integrated programmes. IMS Design and Innovation Academy, Noida offers an integrated programme titled Bachelor in Fashion and Textile Design + Diploma in Fashion and Textile Design. The eligibility criteria differs as per the course and branch selected by the candidate. The courses duration ranges from three to four years depending on the course. UG level : To pursue a Fashion Design course at PG level, aspirants should have completed their graduation with passing marks from a recognised institute in a related field. The courses duration is of two years.
