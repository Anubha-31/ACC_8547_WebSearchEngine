Delhi NCR covers entire National Capital Region (NCR) comprising the neighbouring cities of Delhi – Gurgaon, Ghaziabad, Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, Sonepat, along with Delhi. BTech Colleges in Delhi/NCR offer quality BTech education with high placement record. These colleges offer full-time courses. BTech programme is your gateway to a career in Engineering with a duration of four years. Computer Science Engineering specialisation is the most sought-after and offers bright prospects for student’s growth.

Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for course may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for BTech is as follows:
BTech

Must have passed Class 12 with minimum 60% aggregate from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as core subjects (the pass percentage may vary in different colleges).
Admission is based on merit, written exam, Group Discussion or Personal Interview.

Study Scope after BTech
Students investing in higher education yield long-term benefits. Some students can opt for programmes like MTech/ME and MS while some go on to do business administration courses like MBA.

The most common exam for MBA admission is Common Admission Test (CAT) examination.
