Design is a career stream that can be pursued by candidates at undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) or doctorate (PhD) level to become designers. It offers vast streams and several opportunities to the students from different backgrounds based on their skills and talent. In India, there are around 1,670 colleges which offer Design course. Out of these around 76% are private, approximately 17% are public/government, and around 7% are public/private. Many Design colleges in India offer design courses at different levels. The candidates must fulfil the following eligibility criteria for admission to Design colleges in India: UG Level Candidates must have completed from a recognised board. Class 12th Candidates from any field, i.e. Science, Commerce, and Arts, can apply for the course. PG Level Candidates should have completed from a recognised college/ institute. graduation Most colleges prefer graduates with a Bachelors degree in design stream only.
