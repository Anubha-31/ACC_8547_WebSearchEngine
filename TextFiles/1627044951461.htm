Courses UG Courses PG Courses Top Exams Q&A Articles What is Paramedical? Paramedical courses are job oriented academic programs. Paramedical courses teach an individual and change them into a competent health care worker. There is an increasing realization of the significance of paramedical personnel in the health sector, particularly in trauma and accident care. Health care delivery systems are undergoing fast changes and the demand for expert paramedical personnel is on the rise. This, in turn, has increased the demand for Paramedical courses in recent times. By going through this post you will be able to know about paramedical Careers’ specializations, salary, jobs, and prospects of growth. This career is expected to gain momentum in the coming years because of the increase in the number of corporate hospitals. What if this career does not work out for me? What are the other options for a person with these credentials? After completing the basic course in any of the paramedical streams, the aspirants can take up a job in a Nursing Home, Hospital, Clinics, and Health Departments as well colleges and universities as lecturer, etc. Besides, they can open their own laboratories/clinics. The career prospects for paramedical streams are thus varied. Paramedical Courses in India: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) Q. Which paramedical course is the best? A. A few courses that are in demand are: Bachelor of Physiotherapy, Bachelor of Operation theatre Technology, BSc Nursing, BSc Medical Lab Technology, BSc in Renal Dialysis Technology, BSc in Radiography, BSc in X-Ray Technology and BSc in Anesthesia Technology. Q. What does the paramedical course mean? A. Paramedical courses are those that support medical work but do not require a fully qualified doctor such as first aid, physiotherapy, X-ray processing. These can also be termed as emergency or pre-hospital services. Q. What is the use of a paramedical degree? A. Paramedics help diagnose the disease of a person with the help of blood tests, MRI, X-ray, Ultrasound, etc. Paramedics also assist the doctors in their effort to provide the best treatment available to the patients. Q. Is NEET required for admission to paramedical courses? A. No, there is no need to appear for NEET. Various institutes conduct their own entrance examination for admission to paramedical courses. Some institutes also accept candidates on the basis of the marks scored in class X and XII. Q. What are the paramedical courses that can be opted for after class X? A. All Certificate and Diploma courses can be opted for after completing class X. Popular Paramedical Colleges in India Following are the most popular Paramedical Colleges in India.
