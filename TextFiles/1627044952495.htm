General category candidates have to score 50 per cent or above in the PCB section in Class 12. Q. When will NEET 2021 be conducted? A. NEET 2021 is scheduled to be held on September 12, 2021. Q. According to the notification released by NTA, a candidate can appear for NEET any number of times. There is no restriction on the number of attempts permitted. The exam is conducted once a year. Q. What is the minimum score required in NEET 2021 to get a seat in a government medical college? A. For admission to a government medical college, one must secure at least 600 marks in NEET 2021. Q.
