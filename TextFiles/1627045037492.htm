All India Law Entrance Test (AILET) is conducted by National Law University Delhi () every year to offer admission to eligible candidates in undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG) and doctorate (PhD) level law courses. This is a university-level entrance exam only for admission in . The university does not accept any other law entrance exam for .
