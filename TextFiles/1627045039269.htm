What is AMU Entrance Exam? Aligarh Muslim University Entrance Exam is the entrance test conducted by the university for admissions to undergraduate and postgraduate courses offered by it. AMU conducts two types of entrance tests to offer admission to candidates in different courses: Admission Test wherein exams are conducted at the university level Departmental Test wherein exams are conducted by the respective departments The entrance exam is conducted in pen-paper based test mode for all courses. The candidates qualifying the entrance exam as well as fulfiling the eligibility criteria will be offered final admission. A: As per the university, the admission process for the entrance exam is as under: – Candidates fill and submit the application form of AMU entrance exam in online mode at the official website of amucontrollerexams.com. The candidates also need to pay the application fee as per their courses. Online application – AMU will issue admit cards to candidates who registered and submitted the application form before the last date successfully. The admit card of AMUEE will mention details about the examination, candidates and the allotted test centre name and location. Issuing of admit card – Candidates have to appear for the examination at the allotted test centre as per the examination schedule. Appearing for the test – The University will declare results of AMU entrance exam for each course separately. The results will be available in the form of a merit list of qualified candidates as well as waitlisted candidates. Announcement of results – AMU will conduct the counselling process of each course based on merit lists and waitlists. The candidates fulfilling eligibility criteria, qualifying criteria and other requirements will be allotted seats. A: Yes, for the following courses, it is mandatory for appearing in the AMU entrance exam: - BA (Hons.), BSc (Hons.), BCom (Hons.), BSc (Hons.) Life Sciences, BA LLB (Hons.) courses. A: After the candidates will submit the online application form of AMU entrance exam, a message will be sent on the registered mobile number and email address of the candidates. A: The university offers admissions to various courses such as MA (foreign languages), BFA, MFA, MIRM, MHRM, MSc, BVoc, PGD Business Management, MCom, etc. through Departmental Tests i.e. candidates have to apply and appear for the test conducted by the concerned faculty. Q: What courses are offered through AMU entrance exam? A: The university offers undergraduate courses at faculty of arts, commerce and, agricultural science, science as well as master’s degree in management stream through the entrance exam called AMUEE. Q: Is there any negative marking in AMU entrance exam? A: Yes, there will deduction of 25% per cent or one-fourth of the marks allotted in case of each wrong attempt. For example, a question carries one marks and candidates have answered it incorrectly, then 0.25 marks out of 1 mark will be deducted for this question. Q: What are ‘Non-Test’ courses of AMU? A: Some of the courses offered at faculties of AMU, there will be no admission test/ departmental test. The admissions for these courses will be based solely on qualifying examination. The concerned department/faculty will share the qualifying examination and admission criteria with the applicants only. Q: What is the helpline number of AMU admissions related queries? A: For any queries related to AMU entrance exam/ application forms, candidates can reach out to the exam conducting authority on - 0571-2700935 or they can write a mail on - amu.admission@gmail.com. Q: What is the mode of examination for AMU entrance 2021? A: The entrance exam will be held in pen-paper based mode i.e. candidates need to fill their responses in an OMR sheet only. Q: Where will the AMU entrance exam be held? A: The exam will be held at AMU test centres located in seven cities across India. For the department tests, the exam centre will be in Aligarh only.
