DRDO CEPTAM is conducted by Centre for Personnel Talent Management (CEPTAM) of Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO). CEPTAM conducts the exam for recruitment of Technical, Administrative & Allied category non-gazetted personnel. CEPTAM is a corporate body of DRDO established as Personnel Assessment Centre (PEACE) to cater to DRTC Cadre (Technical Staff) of DRDO. The centre is responsible for the recruitment of Technical, Administrative & Allied category staff, assessment and facilitates training for this cadre. Read More Highlights of DRDO CEPTAM Exam name DRDO CEPTAM Conducted by Centre for Personnel Talent Management (CEPTAM) of DRDO Post MTS, Technician ‘A’, Senior Technical Assistant ‘B’ (STA B) and Admin and Allied Cadre (A&A) Exam level National Frequency of exam Once a year Exam stages Tier 1, 2 and Document Verification Exam mode Online Exam duration For Tech A and A&A posts, Tier-I will be held for 120 minutes For MTS post, Tier-I and II are held for 90 minutes Exam syllabus MTS Post Tier-I comprises questions from General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness and Quantitative Aptitude & Numerical Ability Tier-2 comprises questions from General Science, General Math and General English Tech A comprises questions from Quantitative Ability/Aptitude, General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness, English Language (basic knowledge), General science. Part A comprises questions specific to trade/discipline as per the post-code. Part B Tech A & A The syllabus for Stenographer Grade-II (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing) and Store Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), comprises questions from Quantitative ability/aptitude, General intelligence & Reasoning ability, General awareness, General science, Arithmetic & Numerical Ability, General English and Hindi or English Language. The syllabus for Security Assistant ‘A’, Clerk (Canteen Manager Grade-III), Asstt Halwai-cum Cook, Vehicle Operator ‘A’, Fire Engine Driver ‘A’ and Fireman comprises questions from Quantitative ability/aptitude, General intelligence & Reasoning ability, General awareness, General science, Arithmetic & Numerical Ability and General English and trade-related subjects. When DRDO CEPTAM MTS exam dates will be announced? A. DRDO CEPTAM MTS exam dates will be announced soon. The exam was earlier scheduled to be conducted last year, however; it was postponed by the examination authority due to COVID-19 pandemic. Q. How can I apply for DRDO CEPTAM? A. Candidates need to apply online for DRDO CEPTAM. The application form is released separately for different posts. Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM Tech A recruitment? A. DRDO CEPTAM Tech A recruitment is the exam conducted for the posts such as Carpenter, Draughtsman (Mechanical), Electrician, Fitter, Turner, Welder etc. Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM STA 'B' recruitment? A. The recruitment for DRDO CEPTAM STA ‘B’ is conducted for the post of Senior Technical Assistant ‘B’ under Defence Research & Development Organisation Technical Cadre (DRTC). Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM A&A recruitment? A. DRDO CEPTAM A&A recruitment is conducted for the post of Stenographer Grade-II (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing) and Store Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Security Assistant ‘A’, Clerk (Canteen Manager Grade-III), Asstt Halwai-cum Cook, Vehicle Operator ‘A’, Fire Engine Driver ‘A’ and Fireman. Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM MTS recruitment? A. DRDO CEPTAM MTS recruitment is conducted to fill up the vacant posts of Multi Tasking Staff (MTS), General Central service Group ‘C’, Non-Gazetted, Ministerial post. Q. What is DRDO CEPTAM exam pattern for MTS? A. The exam pattern for MTS post is divided into two Tiers. Tier 1 is for screening while tier 2 is for final selection. Both tier 1 and 2 are conducted in Computer Based Mode. Tier 1 comprises questions from General Intelligence & Reasoning Ability, General Awareness and Quantitative Aptitude & Numerical Ability. Tier 2 consists of questions from General Science, General Math and General English. Q. What are the age criteria for applying to the various posts under DRDO CEPTAM? A. The age limit for DRDO CEPTAM is different for different posts. The age limit for Tech A post is 18 to 28 years. The age limit for MTS post is between 18 and 25 years. The age limit for A&A post is between 18 and 27 years. Q. Is there negative marks in DRDO CEPTAM? A. No, there is no negative marking in DRDO CEPTAM. The scores of candidates are calculated based on the number of correct answers. Q. Is there any qualifying marks in DRDO CEPTAM exam? A. Yes, there are qualifying marks in DRDO CEPTAM exam. The minimum qualifying marks for tier 1 are 40% for UE/ESM/OBC and 35% for SC/ST candidates. Q. What is the required educational qualification for applying to DRDO CEPTAM? A: The educational qualification is different for different posts. For Tech A post, the required educational qualification is Class 10 with certification from ITI in relevant trade. For A&A post, candidates applying to the posts of Clerk, Asstt Halwai-cum Cook, Vehicle Operator, Fire Engine Driver, Fireman must have passed Class 10 while for Stenographer, Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Administrative Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (English Typing), Store Assistant ‘A’ (Hindi Typing), Security Assistant posts, the educational qualification is Class 12. For MTS, candidates must have passed Class 10 or equivalent from a recognised board or Industrial Training Institute (ITI).
