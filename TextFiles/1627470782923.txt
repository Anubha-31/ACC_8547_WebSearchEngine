Top Colleges in Karnataka
Top Engineering Colleges in Karnataka 2021


Karnataka is recognised as the most rapid economic as well as a technologically developed state. The state also includes Bengaluru which is known as a hub for Information Technology. Apart from Bengaluru, many other districts offer a good platform of education including Manipal, Mysore, Mangalore, etc. BTech Colleges in Karnataka offer quality education with high placements. These colleges offer full-time BTech courses in Karnataka that are your gateway to a career in Engineering. These courses are for a duration of four years. Of the many BTech specialisations, Computer Science Engineering is the most sought-after and offers bright prospects for student’s growth.
Karnataka is home to prestigious institutes to pursue a course like NITK Surathkal - National Institute of Technology, Mangalore; Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal; VTU - Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum; Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore; IIIT Bangalore - International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore; RVCE Bangalore - R.V. College of Engineering, Bangalore; BMS College of Engineering, Bangalore; Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur; PES University (PESU), Bangalore; NHCE Bangalore - New Horizon College of Engineering, Bangalore, etc. In Karnataka, BTech degree is offered across various specialisations. Some of the most prominent ones are as follows:
BTech

Aeronautical Engineering
Civil Engineering
Computer Science and Engineering
Automation and Robotics Engineering
Biotechnology Engineering
Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
Electronics and Communication Engineering
Chemical Engineering
Aerospace Engineering
There are a total of nearly 270 engineering colleges in Karnataka which offer a full-time, distance-learning and part-time mode of study. These colleges provide degree courses. Out of these, the top 20 engineering colleges in Karnataka ranked by NIRF offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Some of these colleges offer dual degree specialisations integrated programmes (5-year duration). IIIT Bangalore offers an integrated programme, including BTech + MTech in Computer Science and Engineering. KLE Technological University, Hubli offers dual specialisation in Automation and Robotics Engineering. The National Institute of Engineering, Mysore and PESU, Bangalore offers dual specialisation in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore offers dual specialisation in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. Below listed colleges offer dual specialisation in Computer Science and Engineering:

BTech Admission Eligibility Criteria
The eligibility criteria for BTech course may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for BTech is as follows. The candidates:

Must have passed Class 12 with minimum 60% aggregate from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as core subjects (the pass percentage may vary in different colleges)
Admission is based on merit, written exam, Group Discussion or Personal Interview and it varies from college to college

Study Scope after BTech
Students investing in higher education yield long-term benefits. Some students can opt for programmes like MTech / ME and MS while some go on to do business administration courses like MBA.

Many universities conduct their own admission test but the most common exam for MTech admission is Graduate Aptitude Test for Engineering (GATE) examination
Many students go for an MS degree from a foreign university. To do MS you must clear TOEFL/IELTS (English proficiency tests) and GRE to get scholarships
The most common exam for MBA admission is Common Admission Test (CAT) examination
