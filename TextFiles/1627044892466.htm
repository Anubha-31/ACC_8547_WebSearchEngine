What is Automobile Engineering? Automobile Engineering is the branch of engineering which deals with designing, manufacturing, mechanical mechanisms as well operations of automobiles. It is also an introduction to vehicle engineering which includes cars, motorcycles, trucks and buses etc. Automobile Engineering - Disciplines There are some of the important disciplines that important automotive engineer are as follows Safety engineering Cost Fuel economy/emissions Program timing NVH engineering Assembly feasibility Vehicle electronics Quality management Performance Shift quality Durability/corrosion engineering Drivability Automobile Engineering –Syllabus A year-wise breakup of the course’s syllabus is tabulated below. The automobile industry in India is concentrated in Bengaluru, Pune, Chennai, Gurgaon, Indore, Sanand, and Manesar FAQs related to Automobile Engineering Q. Does a career in Automobile Engineering has a future? A. Yes. Advances in sensor, battery, VLSI, AI, and other technologies are the key drivers for a boost in the automobile sector in India and across the world. Q. What jobs are available for Automobile Engineers? A. Jobs in Automobile engineering are available at vehicle manufacturers, Tier-1 Suppliers, Tier-2 suppliers, Module developers, Tool developers, Test, and Certification labs. More than 500 companies in India directly engaged in design, development, manufacturing & testing of vehicles. Q. What are the job requirements for Automobile Engineers? A. Companies in Automobile Engineering can be classified as vehicle manufactures, electronic subsystem suppliers, software providers, test/certification labs. Requirements from each of these companies will be slightly different, but all of them expect engineers to have an idea of vehicle mechanics, electrical and electronic architecture, embedded controller basics, knowledge of sensors and actuators, Electric power-train and telematics. Q. Are Automobile Engineering jobs available all over India? A. The automobile industry is concentrated in Bengaluru, Pune, Chennai, Gurgaon, Indore, Sanand, and Manesar. Q. What is the typical salary and career growth one can expect in the Automobile sector? A. The automobile will undergo more changes in the next 10 years than it has since its inception 125 years ago. Electric vehicle development is in the transition phase, while new technologies like ADAS, Autonomous driving, Connectivity, Shared mobility are in the development phase. All of these demand huge numbers of skilled & competent engineers. Q. In an adverse economic situation, will I be able to get a job after doing a course in Automobile Engineering? A. No doubt during a downturn in the economy, there is a general impact on all industries including the automobile segment. But companies are always on the lookout for skilled candidates and recruit them irrespective of market conditions. Q. Can I start a company of my own after this course? A. Yes. However, it is strongly advised to gain some real-world experience for a few years/projects and then venture into business.
