A: There is no prescribed validity for the UGC NET eligibility certificate received for the post of Assistant Professor. A: To apply for JRF, candidates should not be more than 30 years in age. There is no age limit to apply for the post of Assistant Professor. Q: Is Aadhaar card mandatory to apply for the UGC NET exam? A: No, you can mention any identification number (PAN card, ration card, passport etc) at the time of filling the UGC NET application form.
