What is BITSAT?
BITSAT 2021 is an entrance exam conducted by the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, for admissions to undergraduate engineering courses (BE) at its three campuses located at Pilani, Goa and Hyderabad. Admission will be offered to the candidates into BE, BPharm and MSc programmes after qualifying for the admission test. The exam is conducted online and around 3 lakh students apply for this entrance exam every year. BITSAT, a computer-based entrance exam, is expected to be conducted in the third or fourth week of June 2021.

FAQs regarding BITSAT 2021

Q. When will the BITSAT 2021 be conducted?
A. BITSAT 2021 will tentatively be conducted from August 3 to 9, 2021.

Q. When is the process of BITSAT 2021 application form filing is likely to begin?
A. BITSAT 2021 application forms are available from February 23, 2021. The last date to fill the application form was July 7.

Q. Is there any provision of direct admission to BITS Pilani and its other campuses?
A. First rank holders of all national and state boards are eligible for direct admission to BE courses offered by the BITS Pilani.

Q. Is the BITSAT score mandatory for B.Pharma admission at BITS campuses?
A. If you are aspiring for BPharma admission in any of the campuses of BITS, you will have to clear the BITSAT exam.

Q. Does any private college (expect BITS) accept a BITSAT score for B.Tech admission?
A. BITSAT score is valid only for BE admission offered by BITS Goa, Pilani and Hyderabad campuses.

Q. Is BITSAT tougher than JEE Main?
A. According to previous trends and experts' feedback, the difficulty level of JEE Main and BITSAT is almost similar.

Q. I have taken Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 10+2. Am I Eligible to Apply for BITSAT?
A. Candidates with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 10+2 can attempt BITSAT 2021. However, they will be eligible to apply only to the BPharm programme.

Q. I passed the class 12 exam in 2020 with less than 75% aggregate in PCM. Am I eligible if I reappeared in the class 12 exam to improve my marks?
A. If the candidates are repeating the class 12 exam, they should do it for all the subjects to pass 12th again as mentioned in the BITSAT brochure. If they have taken improvement exams in PCM/PCB only, they are not eligible.

Q. Are the candidates appearing in the class 12 exams eligible to apply?
A. Yes, candidates appearing for the class 12 exams are eligible to apply for the entrance test.
