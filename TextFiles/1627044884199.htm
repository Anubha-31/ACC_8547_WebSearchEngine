Telangana Top Engineering Colleges in Telangana 2021 Telangana is one of the fastest-growing states in India posing an average annual growth rate of 13.90% over the last five years. There are numerous educational institutions in the state and has ample of opportunities for the students who wish to pursue BTech course. BTech Colleges in Telangana offer quality education with high placements. These colleges offer full-time BTech courses in Telangana that are your gateway to a career in Engineering. These courses are for a duration of four years. Of the many BTech specialisations, Computer Science Engineering is the most sought-after and offers bright prospects for student’s growth. Telangana is home to prestigious institutes to pursue a course like Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Hyderabad; National Institute of Technology (NIT), Warangal; International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Hyderabad; Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTUH), Hyderabad; Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation, Hyderabad; University College of Engineering (Osmania Engineering University), Hyderabad; Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), Hyderabad; VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad. BTech degree is offered across various specialisations. Some of the most prominent ones are as follows: BTech Aeronautical Engineering Computer Science and Engineering Information Technology Electronics and Communication Engineering Electrical and Electronics Engineering Biotechnology There are a total of 260 engineering colleges in Telangana that offer a full-time and distance-learning mode of study. These colleges provide degree courses. Out of these, the top 20 engineering colleges in Telangana ranked by NIRF offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Some of these colleges offer dual degree specialisations. Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad offers dual specialisation in Electronics and Communication Engineering. SNIST, Hyderabad offers dual specialisation in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
