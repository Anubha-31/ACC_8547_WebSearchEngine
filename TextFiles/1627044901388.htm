Before filling out the application form, the candidates are advised to check the UPCET 2021 eligibility criteria. Read to know more about UPCET 2021. What is UPCET 2021? Known earlier as UPSEE, UPCET is a gateway for candidates seeking admission to various professional courses offered at the undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) level at all affiliated institutes of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur and Harcourt Butler Technical University (HBTU), Kanpur. UPCET 2021 will not be valid for BTech and BArch admissions as it will be done on the basis of JEE Main and NATA scores respectively. MBA course admissions in institutes affiliated to AKTU will be done on the basis of CMAT scores. To check the UPCET 2021 admit card, the candidates will have to log in using the required credentials. While visiting the exam centre, the candidates will have to present the UPCET admit card along with identity proof. UPCET 2021 Application Form To participate in the entrance examination, the candidates were required to fill the application form of UPCET 2021. Registrations had started at the official website. No other mode of application was accepted by the authorities. The candidates had to visit the official website and enter their basic details such as name, mobile number, email address, gender and more. Step 1 - Registration: Personal, academic and communication details had to be entered by the candidates. The preferred course and test centre also had to be selected. Step 2 - Filling of Form: The scanned photograph and signature of the candidates had to be uploaded. The scanned documents should have been as per the specifications mentioned by the authorities. Step 3 - Uploading of Documents: Candidates had to pay the required application fee in online mode i.e., net banking/credit card/debit card/UPI. Step 4 - Payment of Fee: Application Fee Category Fee Male/ Transgender Candidates of General/ OBC category Rs 1300 SC/ ST/ Female/ PH Candidates of all categories Rs 650 UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria General UPCET/UPSEE 2021 Eligibility Criteria There is no age limit Candidates should have passed the qualifying exam from any institution in UP or the parents should have the domicile of Uttar Pradesh. If the candidates have not passed the qualifying exam from an institution in UP, then they will be required to produce their parent's domicile certificate. Candidates who do meet the domicile requirements will also be eligible to appear for the exam if they meet the other required criteria. UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria for MCA (Integrated) Candidates should have qualified intermediate examination of U.P. Board or 10+2 level examination or its equivalent with Physics and Mathematics/Statistics/Accountancy as compulsory subjects and one of the optional subjects - Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subjects. Should have obtained at least 45% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together. UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria for BPharm Should have passed (with/without grace) intermediate examination of U.P. Board or 10+2 level examination or its equivalent with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics/Biology subject. UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria for BHMCT/BFAD /BFA/B Voc/ BDes/MBA (Integrated) Candidates must have passed (with/without grace) the intermediate examination of the U.P. Board or the 10+2 level examination or its equivalent. Should have secured a minimum of 45% marks (40% marks for SC/ST category candidates) in aggregate. UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria for MCA in any institution affiliated to AKTU, Lucknow Candidates should have passed BCA/ Bachelor degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent Degree or passed BSc. /BCom/ BA with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at graduation level. Should have scored a minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST). UPCET 2021 Eligibility Criteria for BTech Lateral Entry Candidates who have passed (with/without grace) three years Engineering Diploma from an Institution located in U.P. with minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST) OR whose parents (Father or mother) are domicile of Uttar Pradesh and he/she has passed three years Engineering Diploma with minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST) recognized by the Board of Technical Education, U.P., in any branch of Engineering (except Agriculture Engg., Pharmacy and Architecture). Candidates who have passed (with/without grace) three years B.Sc. degree and passed XII standard with mathematics as one of the subjects from any recognized University; securing minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST) in aggregate. These candidates shall be considered only after filling the supernumerary seats in this category with the candidates belonging to the Diploma stream. Admission to the second year of B.Tech (Lateral entry) of HBTU would be governed by the eligibility criteria laid down by HBTU UPCET 2021 Reservation Criteria Through UPCET 2021, two types of reservations are given to candidates: vertical and horizontal. The secured scores in the examination will be released through the UPCET 2021 result. Candidates will be able to check the result only in online mode. On the basis of the result, UPCET 2021 merit list will be prepared by the authorities. Candidates will be invited for the counselling session based on the merit list. UPCET 2021 Counselling All candidates who are listed in the merit list will be able to appear for UPCET 2021 counselling. NTA will conduct the counselling session in online mode. During the counselling session, candidates will be allotted seats in their respective courses and colleges on the basis of merit. UPCET scores will be taken into consideration during allotment. Is it important to have Aadhaar card to fill UPCET application form? A: Yes, candidates need to enter their Aadhar number in the online application form of UPCET. Q. Is there any age limit for UPCET 2021? A. There is no age limit for admission to the undergraduate and postgraduate courses covered in UPCET 2021. Q. When will the UPCET 2021 correction facility be available? A. UPCET 2021 application form correction facility is available from July 17 to 23. Q. Will the admit card of UPCET 2021 release online? A. The official date for UPCET 2021 admit card has not been announced yet. Q. Who will conduct UPCET 2021? A. National Testing Agency (NTA) will conduct UPCET 2021.
