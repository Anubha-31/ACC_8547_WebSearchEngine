Top Engineering Colleges in Kerala 2021 Kerala, known for having India’s highest literacy rate at nearly 94 per cent, enjoys a unique status of being country’s key education hubs. The state, economy of which contributes nearly four percent to India’s total economy, is now emerging as one of the largest corporate destinations as well making it a perfect choice to study BTech courses. These colleges offer full-time four-year BTech courses in Kerala that van be a career in Engineering. This course is offered in various specialisations and of these Computer Science Engineering is the most sought-after. Kerala has some of the top-rated BTech colleges in India such as National Institute of Technology (NIT), Calicut; Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Thiruvananthapuram; College of Engineering (COE) Trivandrum, Thiruvananthapuram; Government Engineering College, Thrissur; Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), Kochi; Thangal Kunju Musaliar College of Engineering (TKM College), Kollam; Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology (RSET), Kochi, etc. In Kerala, degree is offered across different specialisations. Some of the major ones are as follows: BTech Civil Engineering Computer Science and Engineering Mechanical Engineering Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering Safety and Fire Engineering Chemical Engineering Aerospace Engineering There are a total of 185 engineering colleges in Kerala which offer a full-time, distance-learning and part-time mode of study. These colleges provide degree courses. Out of these, the top 11 engineering colleges in Kerala ranked by NIRF and India Today offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Some of these colleges offer dual degree specialisations. Mohandas College of Engineering and Technology, Thiruvananthapuram, offers dual specialisation in Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering. RSET, Kochi and MEC, Kochi, offer dual specialisation in Computer Science and Engineering. CUSAT, Kochi offers dual specialisation in Safety and Fire Engineering. BTech Admission Eligibility Criteria The eligibility criteria for BTech course is different for different colleges. However, the basic eligibility criteria for BTech is as follows. Candidates must have passed Class 12 with minimum 60% aggregate from a recognised board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as core subjects (the pass percentage may vary in different colleges) Candidates admission is based on merit, written exam, Group Discussion or Personal Interview (GDPI) and is different for different colleges.
