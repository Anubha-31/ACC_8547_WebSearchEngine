Pune also known as ‘Oxford of the East’ as it is an academic centre of national repute and is the most sought-after MBA study destination. Pune has the seventh largest metropolitan economy and the sixth highest per capita income in the country. With its large student population, and a large number of academic and research institutes, Pune has a lot to offer to the MBA aspirants. MBA specialisations in retail, health, financial services, banking, consulting, manufacturing, ITES and in other related domains have made Pune as an academic centre of national repute. Master of Business Administration or MBA is one of the most popular post-graduate programmes in Mumbai. The two-year programme is a gateway to a plethora of job opportunities in the corporate world.
MBA colleges in Pune offer quality MBA education with high placements. More than 100 MBA colleges in Pune offering quality education, make the city a cherished dream of pursuing management education from here. Well known management institutes and universities are located in Pune. Department of Management Sciences (PUMBA), Savitribai Phule Pune University, Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune, SCMHRD Pune, BIMM and many other MBA colleges in Pune have made the city a preferred MBA destination.
MBA in Pune is broadly offered by three types of MBA colleges, namely – IIMs – IIM Nagpur near Pune; University MBA colleges in Pune like PUMBA, SIBM, SCMHRD, BIMM; AICTE Approved PGDM Colleges in Pune like ISB&M, MITSOB, IIMS, Lexicon MILE, NIA Pune, MITCON, ISMR Pune. All these management institutes make Pune a preferred MBA destination. An MBA professional must possess qualities like excellent communication, leadership, problem-solving, management, research-oriented, analytical thinking, eye for detail, goal-oriented & ability to work under pressure, strong mathematical skills, etc.

The eligibility criteria for MBA course may differ from college to college. However, the basic eligibility criteria for MBA is as follows. The candidates:

Candidates must have passed graduation in any discipline or equivalent from a recognised university as a basic eligibility criteria for MBA (full-time).
Most of the institutes follow the minimum score criteria in graduation which is 50 per cent in average or equivalent. For reserved category students, the minimum score in 45 percent in aggregate.
Final-year graduation candidates are also eligible to apply for MBA, provided they present the proof of completion of graduation degree within the duration specified by the institute.
