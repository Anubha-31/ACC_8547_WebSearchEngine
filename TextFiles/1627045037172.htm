Best books for LSAT India 2021 Important Topics to study from LSAT India Syllabus FAQs regarding LSAT India Q. When will LSAT India result 2021 be declared? A: LSAC Global announced the LSAT India 2021 result on the official website on June 17. Q. What is a good LSAT India score? A. Candidates obtaining 80+ percentile can get admissions to top law colleges. LSAC releases LSAT India result as percentile scores calculated on the basis of scores obtained by the candidate and number of candidates appeared for the exam. Q. How LSAT India is different from other law entrance exams such as CLAT and AILET? A. LSAT India is a standardised test with a uniform pattern and single question paper for admissions to five-year integrated LLB courses, three-year LLB course, LLM course or PhD course in Law. Candidates can take the exam for admissions to more than 85 private law colleges in India. Whereas, and AILET have separate question papers for UG and PG courses. Based on CLAT scores, admissions are done at 22 NLUs and accepting colleges. . Through , only NLU Delhi and a few private law colleges in India offer admissions. . CLAT Check the list of NLUs here AILET Know more about CLAT and AILET here Q. When will LSAT India 2021 be held? A. LSAT India 2021 is over now. The LSAC Global council conducted LSAT India 2021 in two sessions. The exam for the first session was conducted on March 25. The exam for next session was held from May 29 onwards on multiple days and slots. The LSAT India 2021 was conducted as a remotely proctored test that aspirants could give from the comfort of their homes. This decision was taken by LSAT India exam conducting body due to COVID-19 pandemic. Q. How to fill LSAT India 2021 application form? A. LSAT India 2021 application form submission process is over now. The LSAT India application forms were released on November 10, 2020 and candidates could fill the form in online mode only. Q. Has LSAC Global changed the exam pattern of LSAT India? A. No, the exam pattern of LSAT India 2021 is the same as in previous years. However, the mode of examination has been changed to 'remotely-proctored online mode' from the Paper-based test. Initially, the pattern was revised with the inclusion of Variable section as well, overall exam duration was increased to 2 hours 55 minutes. Q. What is Variable section of LSAT India 2021? A. The variable section comprising 23 questions based on Analytical Reasoning or Logical Reasoning or Reading Comprehension was earlier added in the exam pattern of LSAT India. However, later it was removed from the question paper Q. How to prepare for LSAT India? A. Candidates can prepare for LSAT India exam by referring to a preparation strategy as well as knowing the exam pattern, syllabus, and study material. Moreover, practising mock tests by LSAC and previous years questions papers for LSAT India is the key to know exactly what to expect and how to attempt the examination. Q. What is LSAT India Super Prep? A. LSAC provides Super Prep book for candidates appearing in LSAT India. The book is a combination of preparation material, test papers, explanations, answer key and other related information. Interested candidates can get the book either while filling up the application form or separately from the official website. Q. Is there a mock test facility available to prepare for the LSAT India exam? A. Yes, LSAC provides online mock test facility for candidates appearing in the examination so that they can develop a familiarity with the test features and prepare well. Q.
