For prelims exam, you need to focus on Reasoning and Maths. Make a list of topics in Reasoning such as blood relation, seating arrangement, direction puzzle, etc and study each topic and then do a lot of practice. For Maths, practice shortcuts, clear basic concepts, practice regularly and learn theorems. For mains, you need to focus on Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, General Awareness, English/Hindi Language and Computer Knowledge. For General Awareness, focus on the latest events of last six months, for English improve your grammar and vocabulary by reading books and through practice, for Computer learn the basics, networking, hardware, software, internet, windows, excel, etc. Q.
