State Bank of India (SBI) every year conducts SBI PO exam to select eligible candidates for the post of Probationary Officer. A large number of candidates apply for the exam, out of which only a few are selected depending on the number of vacancies. SBI PO is considered the most sought-after banking exam in the country. Successful candidates are posted as Probationary Officers in the various branches of the bank. SBI is a prestigious organisation that offers handsome salary and job security to its employees as a result of which lakhs of candidates rush to apply for the various exams conducted by the bank. SBI PO exam comprises three phases-Prelims, Mains and Group Exercises & Interview. SBI PO 2021 Notification SBI PO notification is expected to be released in September/October to recruit eligible candidates for the post of Probationary Officers (PO) in different branches of SBI. Along with the notification, SBI PO dates, online application process, and other details will also be made available. Candidates selected through SBI PO are liable to be posted anywhere in India. All eligible candidates are required to register online for the recruitment process. SBI follows a three-tier selection process for recruitment to the post of PO. Candidates need to clear each exam stage to get shortlisted for the next stage. Final selection is done on the basis of the mains exam and merit. A: SBI PO notification 2021 is expected to be released in September/October by the State Bank of India. Q. What is the age limit for applying to SBI PO exam? A. The age limit for applying to SBI PO is between 21 and 30 years. However, relaxation in the upper age limit was given to candidates belonging to the reserved categories. Q. I have completed graduation in Science stream. Can I apply for SBI PO? A. Yes, candidates from any discipline can apply for the SBI PO exam. Q. I am in the final year of graduation. Can I apply for SBI PO exam? A.Yes, candidates can apply for SBI PO exam provisionally. However, at the time of the document verification, candidates are required to produce their degree certificates. Q: Which is better Bank PO or MBA? A. MBA is not a career. It is a postgraduation course that may prove beneficial in your career. Probationary Officer, on the other hand, is a career. Q. Does graduation marks matter in SBI PO exam? A. There is no minimum percentage of marks in graduation prescribed for applying to SBI PO. Q. How many times can I give SBI PO exam? A. There is capping in the number of attempts in SBI PO exam. General Category candidates can take four attempts. For other categories, the number of attempts is limited to seven. Candidates within the prescribed age limit can apply for the exam. Q. Is there any bond in SBI PO? A. The selected candidates, at the time of joining, will have to execute a bond of Rs 2 lakh to serve the bank for a minimum period of three years. Q. Will the selected SBI PO candidates be on probation? A. Yes, the selected SBI PO candidates will be on probation for two years. Q. What is the career growth as SBI PO? A.
