INI CET 2021 cutoff INI CET 2021 counselling About INI CET The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Delhi conducts INI CET abbreviated as National Importance Combined Entrance Test. The exam is conducted for providing admission into the PG courses such as , , , , and Master of Chirurgie (MCh) offered by eight AIIMS including AIIMS Delhi, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh, and NIMHANS Bengaluru, JIPMER Puducherry and PGI Chandigarh. The exam was conducted for the first time on November 20, 2020. With effect from 2021, all these institutes will admit PG students on the basis of the marks obtained in the INI CET 2021 exam only. Earlier these 11 institutes used to conduct separate exams for admission into PG courses. The examination is conducted twice a year in online mode, one for the January session and the other for the July session.
